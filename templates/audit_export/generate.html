What is ISO 45001?

ISO 45001:2018 is the international standard for Occupational Health & Safety (OH&S) management systems. It requires organisations to demonstrate documented evidence of hazard identification, risk assessment, legal compliance, employee competence, operational controls, performance monitoring, incident investigation and continual improvement. This Evidence Pack compiles all of that evidence from Vigilo in a single, audit-ready ZIP file.
