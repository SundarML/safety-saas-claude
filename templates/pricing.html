FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free plan?
Yes — we offer a free plan for teams that qualify. It's approved on request rather than self-serve to ensure a good fit. Apply here.
What happens when my Trial plan expires?
Your data is preserved. You'll be prompted to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using Vigilo. We never delete your records — upgrades are instant and your team can pick up exactly where they left off.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
What counts as a "user"?
Any person with an account in your organisation — managers, safety officers, observers, action owners, and contractors. Worker accounts (no-email, PIN-based) also count toward your user limit.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Vigilo is hosted on AWS (Mumbai region) with encrypted data at rest and in transit. Each organisation's data is fully isolated — no data is shared between organisations.
Do you offer a demo before I sign up?
Yes — request a demo and our team will walk you through the platform with your use case in mind. Typically scheduled within 1–2 business days.
